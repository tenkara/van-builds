## ELECTRICAL SYSTEM - BALANCED SOLAR + SHORE POWER

### **Power Requirements (Estimated Daily Use)**

**120V AC Loads (Inverter):**
- Induction cooktop: 1800W × 0.5hr = 900Wh
- Instant Pot: 1200W × 0.5hr = 600Wh
- Microwave/oven/air fryer: 1500W × 0.5hr = 750Wh
- Laptop/phone charging: 100W × 3hr = 300Wh
- **Subtotal AC:** ~2,550Wh/day (212Ah @ 12V)

**12V DC Loads:**
- Fridge (90L): 50W × 24hr = 1,200Wh (100Ah)
- LED lighting: 30W × 5hr = 150Wh (12.5Ah)
- Water pump: 50W × 0.25hr = 12.5Wh (1Ah)
- Diesel heater: 50W × 8hr = 400Wh (33Ah)
- Fans, USB, misc: 100Wh (8Ah)
- **Subtotal DC:** ~1,862Wh/day (155Ah @ 12V)

**TOTAL DAILY: ~4,412Wh (367Ah @ 12V)**

### **Battery System - Lithium (LiFePO4)**
- **Capacity:** 400-600Ah @ 12V (5,000-7,500Wh usable)
- **Configuration:**
  - **Option 1:** 4× 100Ah batteries (400Ah total)
  - **Option 2:** 2× 300Ah batteries (600Ah total)
- **Brands:** Battle Born, Victron, Renogy, RELiON, SOK
- **Days of autonomy:** 1.5-2 days without charging
- **Location:** Under bed, accessible via removable panels
- **Weight:** 120-180 lbs (lithium)

### **Solar System - ROOF + PORTABLE EXPANSION**

**Roof-Mounted Solar:**
- **Panels:** 400-600W total
  - 4× 100W flexible OR
  - 2× 200W rigid OR
  -3× 175W rigid (Renogy, Victron, Rich Solar)
- **Mount:** Low-profile roof racks or bonded (3M VHB + Sikaflex)
- **Tilt:** Flat or minimal tilt (aero, stealth)
- **Location:** Roof, forward of MaxxFan vents
- **Daily yield:** 1,600-2,400Wh (sunny day, 4-6 hrs equivalent)

**Portable Solar Expansion:**
- **Panels:** 200-400W portable suitcase panels
  - Examples: Renogy 200W suitcase, Rockpals 300W, EcoFlow portable
- **Connection:** Anderson connector OR MC4 port (exterior starboard side)
- **Storage:** Under bed OR rear gear garage
- **Use case:** Boost charging when parked for extended periods
- **Combined yield:** Up to 3,000-4,000Wh/day with roof + portable

**Solar Charge Controller:**
- **Type:** MPPT (Maximum Power Point Tracking)
- **Size:** 30-50A (roof) + 20-30A (portable expansion input)
- **Brands:** Victron SmartSolar, Renogy Rover, Epever
- **Features:** Bluetooth monitoring, temperature compensation

### **Inverter/Charger**
- **Size:** 2000-3000W pure sine wave
- **Type:** Inverter/charger combo (for shore power charging)
- **Options:**
  - **Victron MultiPlus 12/3000:** 3000W, 120A charger
  - **Renogy 3000W:** Budget-friendly
  - **Aims 2000W:** Compact
- **Pass-through:** Shore power passes through when connected
- **Location:** Under bed, ventilated compartment

### **Shore Power**
- **Inlet:** 30A RV-style inlet (exterior, driver side rear)
- **Uses:**
  - Battery charging (bulk charging 1-3 hrs)
  - Running high-draw appliances without inverter load
  - Air conditioning (if added in future)
- **Circuit:** Separate 120V AC distribution panel

### **DC-DC Charger (Alternator Charging)**
- **Size:** 50-60A
- **Type:** Renogy DCC50S, Victron Orion, CTEK D250SE
- **Function:** Charges house batteries while driving
- **Input:** Vehicle alternator (smart alternator compatible)
- **Yield:** 600-720W, ~3,000Wh per 5-hour drive day

### **Battery Monitor**
- **Type:** Victron BMV-712 OR Renogy battery monitor
- **Display:** Bluetooth app + optional display panel
- **Data:** SOC%, voltage, current, time remaining

### **Electrical Summary**
- **Total system capacity:** 5,000-7,500Wh
- **Daily generation (solar):** 1,600-4,000Wh (roof + portable)
- **Daily generation (driving):** 3,000Wh (5 hrs driving)
- **Shore power:** Backup/fast charging
- **Autonomy:** 1.5-2 days without sun or driving

## PLUMBING SYSTEM

### **Fresh Water**
- **Tank:** 20-25 gallons (food-grade polyethylene)
- **Location:** Under bed (passenger side or center)
- **Fill:** Exterior gravity fill port + interior fill option (removable)
- **Pump:** 12V demand pump, 3.5-5.0 GPM (Shurflo, Flojet)
- **Lines:** PEX tubing (1/2" main, 3/8" branch)
- **Filter:** Inline sediment + carbon filter
- **Winterization:** Drain valve + bypass for RV antifreeze

### **Hot Water**
- **Option 1:** Diesel heater loop (heat exchanger in fresh tank)
- **Option 2:** On-demand propane (Eccotemp, Camplux)
- **Option 3:** 120V electric tank (6-10 gal, shore power only)
- **Preferred:** Diesel heater loop (uses existing heater)

### **Grey Water**
- **Tank:** 20-25 gallons (ABS or polyethylene)
- **Location:** Under chassis (professionally installed)
- **Inputs:** Sink drain + shower drain
- **Drain:** Gravity dump valve (3" gate valve)
- **Monitor:** Tank level sensor (3-stage: empty/half/full)

### **Toilet**
- **Type:** Dry flush cassette (Laveo Dry Flush OR Thetford Porta Potti Curve)
- **Capacity:** 5 gallons (15-18 uses)
- **Cartridges:** Disposable bags/cartridges
- **Access:** Exterior cassette door (port side)
- **No black tank required**

## CLIMATE CONTROL

### **Heating - Diesel Air Heater**
- **Type:** Diesel air heater (Espar/Eberspächer Airtronic OR Chinese diesel heater)
- **Size:** 2-4kW (adjustable output)
- **Fuel:** Diesel from main tank OR separate 2-3 gal tank
- **Ducting:**
  - Main vent: Living area (under galley or wet bath)
  - Branch: Bed area (under platform or wall vent)
  - Optional: Cab area
- **Control:** Digital thermostat (12V)
- **Power:** 12V DC (10-30W when running)
- **Exhaust:** Exterior exhaust port (underbody)
- **Intake:** Exterior air intake (underbody)

### **Cooling - Ventilation**
- **Roof Fans (2):**
  - **Fan 1:** MaxxAir Deluxe (over bed area, reversible, 10-speed)
  - **Fan 2:** MaxxAir Deluxe (over galley/wet bath, reversible)
  - Rain covers for operation in rain
  - Remote control options
- **Window Ventilation:** Crack windows (if equipped with opening windows)
- **Cross-breeze:** Open front windows + rear door + roof fans
- **Optional:** 12V oscillating fans (portable)

### **Air Conditioning (Future Option)**
- **Not included in base build**
- **Options if added later:**
  - Dometic RTX 2000 (rooftop, 120V AC, ~15-18A)
  - Zero Breeze portable (12V/120V, less efficient)
  - Requires significant power (shore or large solar/battery system)

### **Insulation**
- **Walls:** Spray foam (closed-cell, R-value 10-13) OR rigid foam board + Thinsulate
- **Ceiling:** Spray foam (closed-cell, R-value 13-19) OR Thinsulate (best for curved ceiling)
- **Floor:** 1" rigid foam board (XPS or polyiso, R-5 to R-7) + 1/2-3/4" plywood subfloor
- **Doors:** Reflectix OR foam board inserts (removable for summer)
- **Thermal bridging:** Minimize metal-to-interior contact
- **Sound deadening:** Noico or Dynamat on bare metal before insulation
