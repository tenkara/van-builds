## CLIMATE CONTROL

### **Heating - Engine Coolant Heat Exchanger (SAME SYSTEM AS HOT WATER!)**

**Option: Add Hydronic Heating Loop to Hot Water System**
- **How it works:**
  - Same heat exchanger system that heats water also heats glycol loop
  - Glycol circulates through baseboard heaters or radiator + fan
  - While driving: Free heat from engine
  - While parked: 120V element heats water/glycol
- **Components:**
  - Heat exchanger tank (dual-purpose: hot water + heating)
  - Glycol circulation pump (12V)
  - Baseboard heaters OR small radiator with 12V fan
  - Thermostat control
- **Power:** 12V pumps (~30W), 120V element when parked (750-1000W)
- **Zones:** Living area, bed area

**Alternative: 12V Electric Heaters (Simple)**
- **Ceramic heaters:** 300-500W (12V from EcoFlow)
- **Diesel heater:** If you reconsider (Espar, Webasto) - most efficient
