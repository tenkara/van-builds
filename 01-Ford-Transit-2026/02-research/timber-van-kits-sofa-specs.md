### FMVSS Certification Status
**Important:** Based on research, Timber Van Kits' seat bases are **NOT tested or certified** in Canada or the USA for FMVSS compliance.

- Timber Van Kits recommends checking with local laws and insurance companies before purchase
- They will not be responsible for any injury, death, or losses sustained by the use of these seat bases
- For FMVSS-certified solutions, consider alternative manufacturers

## Installation Requirements Checklist

### Before Purchase
- [ ] Verify FMVSS certification (207 for seats, 210 for seat belt anchorages)
- [ ] Get installation technical drawings from manufacturer
- [ ] Confirm compatibility with Ford Transit 148" ELWB
- [ ] Check insurance requirements for aftermarket seating
- [ ] Verify local/state regulations

### During Installation
- [ ] Professional installation of seat belt anchorages required
- [ ] Mounting must be to vehicle frame or reinforced floor
- [ ] Load testing may be required (14,000+ lbs per FMVSS 210)
- [ ] Documentation of professional installation for insurance

### Positioning Guidelines
Based on typical van conversion practice:
- **From Partition:** As close as installation instructions allow (typically 0-6" gap)
- **From Driver Wall:** Close proximity to maximize aisle space on passenger side
- **Sliding Door Clearance:** Ensure sofa doesn't block sliding door operation
- **Window Alignment:** Consider window views for seated passengers
